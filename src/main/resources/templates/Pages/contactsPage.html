Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Who are we?
    We are Ludek, a Polish community group in South Wales who aim to bring the Polish people of wales together.
  • What do we do?
    Our main aim to help the Polish people in South Wales by organising events, offering help and support as well as connecting families and communities together
  • What type of events do we hold?
    From Polish fairs to dinners and balls, we aim to try include everyone in our events, we want to bring the community together and make everybody feel at home.
  • How can I attend these events?
    By viewing our events section on the website, you can see which events are upcoming, all we ask is that you turn up and enjoy yourself.
  • Is it just South Wales that we operate?
    Our main focus is South Wales, however we also connect to surrounding cities, such as Gloucester and North Wales with their communities also.
  • What resources do you guys provide?
    Our aim is to help anybody who may have any problems or troubles to see them fixed. We provide a range of information spanning from work and housing, to education and support. Helping the community in any way we can is our objective
  • Is there a membership?
    There is no membership, however you are able to sign up for updates and newsletters containing important information about events and recent news.
  • How do I join?
    On our website you can sign up using an e-mail address and phone number
  • Is the information provided reliable?
    Our main source is the government website along with any other reliable sources. The information and help we want to give is authentic and of the highest accuracy.
