Health And Social Care Information

This page is dedicated to providing you with the vital role of health and social care services in supporting the well-being and integration of migrants in wales and the UK. Having this easy access to healthcare can help to feel supported and integrated in new communities. This page covers the crucial and diverse healthcare needs and support mechanisms, including the importance of tailored services and any challenges one may be facing when dealing with the healthcare system. The importance of mental health and the need for compassionate care is also vital, so we hope to provide useful information to make healthcare easy, more accessible and welcoming for all.


Mental Health services in South Wales

Taking care of your mental health is just as important as your physical. This section provides the support and resources, whether you’re seeking professional help, community support, or simple ways to improve your well-being, we’re here to guide you. Together, we can make sure you feel supported and empowered to build a happy and healthy life here.

The link provided will direct you to the Trauma Framework Cymru which fosters trauma-informed support across Wales. The info they provide can enhance awareness, promote sensitive support, and connect people with trauma-informed resources, empowering individuals to overcome obstacles, mental challenges and build resilience in their new environment.

Immediate crisis support

If you or someone you know is in a severe crisis, immediate danger or needs urgent help, don't hesitate to seek support straight away. Here are some services to contact when necessary.

  • 111: For urgent health support of all types and advice.
  • Samaritans: Call 116-123 for 24/7 support if you need someone to talk to.
  • 999: For emergencies where there is an immediate risk to life.
